Common questions

Answers to what testers usually ask.

What happens when the beta ends?

You get six months of Pro features free as thanks for your feedback. After that, you can stay on Pro at the standard rate, downgrade to Lite, or cancel with no obligation.

Is my facility's data safe?

Every organization's data is isolated at the database layer. Your team can only see your work orders, your assets, your team. Nothing is shared between facilities. We don't sell data, ever.

What if the app breaks?

Tell us. We fix it. You have direct access to the founder during beta, so bugs get triaged fast. That's part of what beta means.

Do I need a credit card to sign up?

Yes, but you won't be charged during the beta or the six months after. The card is on file for when you decide to continue as a paying customer. Cancel anytime with no obligation.

Can my whole team join?

Yes. Lite includes up to 10 team members. Invite your techs, they get their own accounts and can update work orders from their phones.
